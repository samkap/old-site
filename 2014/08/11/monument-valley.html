Monument Valley

Monument Valley is easily one of the best games1 I’ve ever played. Every part of the game is perfection, and every detail is delightful and soothing. Even more, the Monument Valley Soundtrack (below) is absolutely stunning. It’s been out on iTunes for a while, but was just added today on Rdio, Spotify, and others according to Jason Kottke.

If you haven’t already played the game by digital product studio, UsTwo, download it ASAP. It is worth every penny. The devs behind the game are working on the next level of stages, and I’ve found myself in a whole new place with my (excited) impatience.
