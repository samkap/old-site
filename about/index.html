Blog Clips About

Hi! I’m Sam Kapila. I’m a designer, educator, and person living in Austin, TX with two cats and a lot of coffee. I’m the Director of Instruction at The Iron Yard, an immersive code and design school with over 20 locations around the US. I am constantly tinkering with responsive web design, web typography, podcasting, writing, speaking about design, and cooking. I grew up on the Dutch island of Curaçao, and have lived in India, Florida, Greece, and South Carolina.
