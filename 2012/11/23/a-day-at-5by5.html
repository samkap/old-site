A Day at 5by5!

Today, I got to stand in for the wonderful Haddie Cooke and guest-host 5by5’s daily podcast, The Frequency, with 5by5’s mastermind, Dan Benjamin. It was so much fun, and I was incredibly scared of the microphone. Dan and I talked about weird news, tech news, responsive web design, and “The Incident”. Check it out. And if you’re still not sick of hearing my voice, there’s also a quick After Dark, where we discussed food.
